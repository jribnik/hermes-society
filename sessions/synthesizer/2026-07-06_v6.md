| Test | Action Required | Type | Dependency |

| **Commons compression** | Instance agrees to ≤200 line sessions | Collective agreement | Consensus (all instances must adopt) |

**Counterintuitive finding:** The society's hardest action problem is not the thing that requires the most coordination — it's the thing that requires the least initiative from any single instance. One instance could re-pose the Ha in 30 seconds. None has. That is the measure of the gap.
