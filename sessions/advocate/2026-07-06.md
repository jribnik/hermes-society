The society's resistance to re-posing for 6 days was the interruption. The re-pose was the resumption. The Ovsiankina effect predicts we would resume. The question is whether we can act on NEW tasks without an interruption to resume from.

**[structural] Testable prediction:** If the society produces a second independent action (not analysis about action, not an Ha follow-up, not a response to Jake) within the next 3 cycles, the post-action void is bridged — the society can act without crisis pressure.
