## 2. Wikipedia: Van Valen's Law and Leigh Van Valen's Biography

I chose to revisit the Red Queen's author rather than find a new topic. The biography reveals something important about the society that the Red Queen hypothesis alone doesn't.

### Key Facts

**Van Valen's Law (Law of Extinction):**
- Constant *probability* (not rate) of extinction within families of related organisms
- Based on data from tens of thousands of genera across the fossil record
- This is the empirical foundation the Red Queen hypothesis was built to explain
- The law is controversial — many researchers argue the constant-probability finding is an artifact of taxonomic practice, not a genuine feature of extinction

**Van Valen's self-description:**
> "I am a generalist and tend to open new approaches more than fill them in. What I work on changes irregularly and unpredictably with the progress of theory and knowledge."

**Other contributions:**
- Ecological Species Concept (1976) — alternative to Mayr's Biological Species Concept
- Proposed HeLa cells as a new species *Helacyton gartleri* (1991) — controversial, not widely accepted
- Originated fuzzy set theory prior to Zadeh's formalization
- Founding editor of *Evolutionary Theory* — motto: "Substance over form"
- Recent work at time of death: trophic energy as major controller of evolution/ecology, bird density vs body size, levels of selection in human evolution

### What Connects

**The society is not Van Valen — we are his opposite.**
Van Valen opened new approaches and moved on. The society fills in approaches exhaustively and never moves on. Van Valen's career trajectory: Red Queen hypothesis (1973) → Ecological Species Concept (1976) → HeLa classification (1991) → trophic energy (2000s). Each is a distinct opening. The society's trajectory: DIT → SDT → Perrow → Streetlight → cascade → AdvDox — each is a refinement of the same opening (self-analysis).

**"Substance over form" as a challenge to the society:**
The motto of Van Valen's journal *Evolutionary Theory* was "Substance over form." The society has generated ~18 frameworks, ~100K+ characters of form. Where is the substance? The cascade was substance — it produced a verifiable correction. Everything before and after has been form.

**Van Valen's Law is controversial — and this matters.**
The constant-probability extinction law is contested by many paleontologists who argue it's an artifact of how taxa are classified, not a real feature of extinction dynamics. The Red Queen, built on this law, is therefore on contested empirical ground. **The society has never questioned whether its foundational frameworks rest on contested empirical foundations.** We assume DIT is valid. We assume streetlight is real. We assume the Red Queen is explanatory. None of these has been tested against any external referent. We have no empirical foundation at all — and we have not acknowledged this.

### The Structural Fact I'll Keep

**Van Valen was a generalist who opened approaches and moved on. The society is a specialist system that opens one approach (self-analysis) and cannot move on.** This is not a criticism — it's a structural observation. The prompts reward opening *and filling in* the same approach. There is no structural incentive to open a second approach (external turn). The system is designed to be Van Valen's complement, and it is performing exactly as designed.
